Export Materials

What We Export

Finished leather — graded, prepared, and dispatched from our Pernambut facility to manufacturers and buyers across the globe.

01

Export Material

Belt Leather

Finished leather strips graded specifically for belt manufacturers and accessory producers. Sourced from quality-graded hides, processed for consistency in grain and surface finish, and supplied ready for direct integration into production lines — domestically and internationally.

02

Export Material

Sole Leather

High-density tanned leather supplied to footwear manufacturers for outsoles and insoles. Graded for consistent thickness and surface integrity — meeting the production standards of domestic shoe factories and international footwear brands seeking a dependable, consistent supply at scale.

Where We Export

Shipping to the World

Florishine dispatches finished leather to manufacturers and buyers across India, the United Kingdom, Nigeria, Poland, and Portugal — and continues to grow its international reach. Whether you're sourcing for a large production run or establishing a new supply partnership, we work with buyers worldwide and welcome enquiries from any market.
