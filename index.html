OUR STORY

Built for the
Global Leather Market

Florishine International Private Limited was founded in 2022 in Pernambut, Tamil Nadu — one of India's foremost leather manufacturing regions. We were established with a single, clear mission: to connect India's manufacturing strength with the global demand for premium finished leather goods and export-grade leather materials.

We operate across the full trade spectrum — manufacturing finished goods at our Pernambut facility while maintaining active export partnerships with buyers in Italy, the United Kingdom, Nigeria, and across domestic India. Every relationship we build is grounded in reliability, transparency, and a commitment to consistent quality at scale.

At Florishine, we don't just trade leather — we build partnerships that last.

Our Presence

Two Continents,
One Craft

Florishine International operates across two of the world's most respected leather regions — rooted in Pernambut's manufacturing strength and present in Venice's tanning heritage — to deliver quality without compromise, from source to finished product.

Pernambut, India

Where It All Begins

Our operations are rooted in Pernambut, Tamil Nadu — one of India's foremost leather manufacturing regions. Every product is crafted here by skilled artisans who bring precision and scale to belts, gloves, garments, and finished leather goods built to international standards.

Venice, Italy

Our European Presence

Venice sits at the heart of Italy's leather heritage. Our European presence gives Florishine direct access to the world's finest tanning traditions — and keeps us close to our international partners, positioned to deliver with consistency across continents.

Export & Global Reach

We Export to the
Global Market

Florishine exports premium finished leather to international buyers and manufacturers worldwide. Our two primary export grades — Belt Leather and Sole Leather — are graded, prepared, and dispatched directly from our Pernambut facility to partners across Europe, West Africa, and domestic India.
